UNIVERSITY LIFE

Life @MBU is a dynamic and transformative experience that combines academic pursuits, personal growth, social interactions, and the development of lifelong skills and friendships. Students feel it as a time of exploration, discovery, and laying the foundation for future endeavours.

Student club

In MBU student clubs have been established with an aim to encourage and promote creativity and thoughtful ideas of the students. The activities of the club are expected to stimulate the pursuit of scientific knowledge, encourage the students potentiality to express scientific ideas and identify their field of interest in science and Technology. Mentoring support is provided for the students to build their ideas into real time products.

Social life

Our Pro-Chancellor started a social unit called Army Green for promoting environmental awareness. It adopted small settlements in the vicinity of the Sree Vidyanikethan Institutions.

Mohan Mantra - Cultural Event

Mohana Mantra is the annual National Level Techno-Cultural fest hosted by Vidyanikethan Educational Trust. It's a stage that brings enthusiastic students together in an environment of competition. The event has grown into one of the biggest educational fests in the country that is a coming together of professional contests and fun-filled events, solely to promote and inculcate the spirit of competition. The programme also has various groups from across the country conducting workshops, seminars and programmes sharing and exchanging knowledge

The Campus Experience

MBU has a wide range of student organizations and clubs catering to various interests, hobbies, and academic disciplines. These groups offer opportunities for students to explore their passions, develop leadership skills, and engage in extracurricular activities. Students organize events, workshops, and competitions that enrich their overall experience

Ncc

National Cadet Corps (NCC), a Youth wing of the Indian Armed Forces. At Mohan Babu University, the Senior Division, NCC unit under 29th Battalion, Army wing works majorly in developing the students character, strength and poster leading them towards the patriotic citizens of India. The major attribute of NCC at Mohan Babu University is discipline, we reform our cadets as the role models of the nation.

Nss

The Motto of NSS "Not Me But You", reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for self-less service. NSS helps the students develop appreciation to other person's point of view and also show consideration to '/other living beings. The philosophy of the NSS is well doctrine in this motto, which underlines/on the belief that the welfare of an individual is ultimately dependent on the welfare of the society overall and therefore, the NSS volunteers shall strive for the well-being of the society.
